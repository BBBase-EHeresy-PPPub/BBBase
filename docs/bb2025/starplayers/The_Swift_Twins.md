| 7  | 3  | 2+ | 3+ | 9+ |

*(Blitzer, Elf)*

* [Block]
* [Loner] (4+)
* [Mighty Blow]
* [Tackle]
* **Working In Tandem**

The Swift Twins must be hired as a pair. Additionally, if Lucien performs a Block Action against an opposition player who is also Marked by Valen, Lucien may re-roll a single Block Dice.
