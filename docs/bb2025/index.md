# PLAYING BLOOD BOWL

![](media/core_rules/blood_bowl_set.jpg)

Blood Bowl is, without a doubt, the greatest game ever played in the Old World - just ask any of its legions of fans who will animatedly tell anyone who will listen just how good it is! Tactical, challenging and outrageously funny, Blood Bowl is a fantasy football game like no other, that will keep coaches coming back for more.

To play a game of Blood Bowl there are a few key items you will need. Before diving into the world of Blood Bowl and exploring the rules of the game further, letâ€™s take a look at a game in action and list the vital components that are required:

1. **BLOOD BOWL PITCH:** This is a foldout cardboard playing surface, marked out to emulate a pitch that might be found in the Blood Bowl world.
2. **TWO DUGOUTS:** These are used to keep track of game information, as well as providing somewhere to place reserves and injured players.
3. **TWO TEAMS OF PLAYERS:** There are many different teams available from Games Workshop, representing the many different races that play this great game.
4. **TEAM MARKERS:** These are used to keep track of game information on your team dugout.
5. **DICE:** Blood Bowl uses multiple different types of dice - two regular six-sided dice, three block dice, an eight-sided dice and a 16-sided dice are all used to bring the action to life.
6. **RULERS AND TEMPLATES:** These are used for passing the ball, determining where the ball lands or bounces to, and so on.
7. **TOKENS:** These are used to denote various in-game effects, such as whether a player is prone, distracted by another effect, and so on.
8. **THE RULEBOOK:** The one you are holding, to be precise! In this book you will find all the rules for the game of Blood Bowl.
9. **QUICK REFERENCE CARDS:** The Blood Bowl starter set contains two Quick Reference Cards; a useful tool to remind you of the game sequence and enable you to quickly access frequently used tables.
